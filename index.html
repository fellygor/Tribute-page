MAU MAU UPRISING


ABOUT

The Mau Mau Uprising is a war that took place between 1952 and 1960. It was also known as the Mau Mau rebellion, the Kenya Revolt or the Mau Mau Revolt. The war was mainly between the British authorities and the Kenya Land Freedom Army.


The leader of this rebellion was Field Marshal Dedan Kimathi. The revolt was mainly demonstrated by Kikuyu, Embu and Meru people. However, it also comprised of both the Kamba and Maasai.


The Mau Mau revolt initiated the decolonization of Kenya from Britain by facilitating the transition from the period of colonization to independence.
